📊 Student Performance Analysis
📌 Overview

In this project, I explored a dataset of student records to understand what affects their final grades (G3). I looked at factors like study time, gender, and other background details to see if they have any impact on performance.

🛠️ Tools Used
Python
Pandas (for handling data)
Matplotlib (for creating graphs)
📂 About the Dataset

The dataset includes information such as:

Basic student details (age, gender, school)
Study time and habits
Family and health background
Past and final grades (G1, G2, G3)
🔍 What I Did
Cleaned the data by removing missing values and duplicates
Found the average final grade
Counted how many students scored above 15
Plotted a histogram to understand grade distribution
Checked if study time affects performance
Compared average scores between male and female students
📊 Key Findings
The average final grade is around 10.4
About 40 students scored above 15
Study time has a very weak relationship with grades
There is a small difference in performance between genders
📈 Visualization

I used a histogram to visually understand how student grades are distributed.

✅ Conclusion

From this analysis, it’s clear that student performance is influenced by multiple factors. Study time alone doesn’t strongly determine the final result, so other aspects also play an important role.
